## overview

exploring single-event upset (SEU) mitigation strategies in digital designs. fault injection, error-correcting codes, and redundant architectures.

## motivation

as designs shrink and operate at lower voltages, bit flips from radiation and noise become harder to ignore. understanding fault tolerance from the RTL level up.

## goals

- fault injection framework for RTL simulations
- evaluation of TMR, ECC, and hardened flip-flop techniques
- practical guidelines for SEU-hardened design

## current status

exploring the problem space. reading up on SEU mitigation literature and existing fault injection tools before committing to an approach.

## roadmap

1. literature review
2. fault injection infrastructure
3. case studies on real designs
4. write-up of findings
